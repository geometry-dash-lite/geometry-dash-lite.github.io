Geometry Dash Lite

Geometry Dash Lite game is a platform game with addictive gameplay, overcome obstacles that come across you with your reflexes while advancing in different levels. You must control the character, which is a small shape, and proceed without getting stuck in difficult obstacles. Each level is full of obstacles with geometric shapes that require careful timing. While colorful and animated backgrounds increase the energy of the game, the complexity of the obstacles will challenge you a lot. The music of the game keeps the rhythm and excitement of the game at a high level as it progresses in synchronization with the gameplay. Therefore, speed, reflexes and correct timing are critical for completing the levels.
